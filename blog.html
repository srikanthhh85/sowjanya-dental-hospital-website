Your Daily Smile Routine

Morning Care

  • Brush thoroughly for two minutes
  • Use fluoride toothpaste
  • Clean your tongue
  • Rinse with an antiseptic mouthwash

Evening Care

  • Floss before brushing
  • Brush gently but thoroughly
  • Avoid eating after cleaning
  • Use a night guard if prescribed

The Role of Diet in Dental Health

What you eat affects your smile. Consider:

Smile-Friendly Foods

  • Crunchy fruits and vegetables
  • Calcium-rich dairy products
  • Sugar-free beverages
  • Lean proteins

Foods to Limit

  • Sugary snacks and drinks
  • Acidic beverages
  • Sticky candies
  • Hard foods that may crack teeth
